Three Pillars of Purpose

🌱

Language Preservation

Of the world's ~7,000 languages, over half are endangered. Kokborok faces pressure from dominant languages in digital spaces. NLP tools create lasting digital artifacts — corpora, annotated datasets, and models — that actively sustain the language in the digital age.

🌐

Digital Inclusion

When a language has no NLP support, its speakers are excluded from modern technology — no autocomplete, no search, no voice assistants, no translation. This project is a first step toward giving Kokborok speakers access to the same AI-powered tools enjoyed globally.

🔬

NLP Research Frontiers

Building NLP systems for low-resource languages pushes transfer learning, cross-lingual models, and data-efficient techniques to their limits — advancing the entire discipline toward a richer, more inclusive AI ecosystem.

"A language is not just words — it is a way of seeing the world. When a language disappears, an entire universe of knowledge, metaphor, and human experience is lost forever."
