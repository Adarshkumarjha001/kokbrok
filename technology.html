Design Choice

Why XLM-RoBERTa?

🌍

100 Languages

XLM-RoBERTa was pre-trained on 100 languages. Its cross-lingual representations transfer well to low-resource Kokborok.

🧩

Subword Tokenization

SentencePiece BPE handles unseen and agglutinative morphology naturally — ideal for Kokborok's complex word-building patterns.

🎯

Efficient Fine-Tuning

A small Kokborok annotated corpus is sufficient for effective fine-tuning — no need for millions of labeled examples.
